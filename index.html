Description: The purpose of our project is to provide a platform for university students to connect with extracurricular organizations. Our application allows for students to easily access clubs and organizations that are both friendly in terms of UI and UX. Our app is specifically geared towards UNC students. Students can easily check the app for activities going on around campus, without needing to pull out laptops or searching through various social media platforms.

Problems: UNC students have limited opportunities other than annual Fall fest to connect with various clubs/resources on campus. Heel life has only a website version, which is not instant enough. There is no existing mobile platform for student clubs to announce or advertise for their events, resources, and opportunities.

Function: Account for each user, organization account, messaging between user & organization, hold/house organization bylaws, rosters, descriptions, links, info on how to join, dues, meeting frequency, View images from organization.
